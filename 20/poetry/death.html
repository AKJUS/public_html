Death
by: Mind Booster

Man allways persuits
it's dream of eternity...
Is it fear?
Fear of death?

All the day,
every day,
you see...
Things that show the will
of live forever...

We live in a dream,
we love a dream...
Live forever!

We want to live
in a dream,
and dream forever...

The few who don't,
live fascinated
seeing other's dream...

The few who don't
are the true ones
who don't fear death.

You ask your god,
or science, or technology,
to fullfill your dream,
your imagination...

And then you fear...
And then you dream...
And then you die.
