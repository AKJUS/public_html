EN

Hi. I'm marado, and my longest available tilde appeared in 1999, and is still online. However, in 2005, predicting that I would loose access to it, I started redirecting its e-mail and webpage to a more updated place. Currently, it redirects to the weblog I created in 2005, exactly to replace my webpage. What a mistake. Fifteen years later, I learned about the tildeverse, and you won't believe how glad I was with it: returning to the good old days of an easilly maintainable web presence. You can, of course, keep in touch with me in ways that get more regularly updated than this webpage: just take a look at the links section.
